## Confluence 

Confluence is a part of Atlassian suite of products. It is a sophisticated and versatile collaborative documentation tool. Confluence provide a way for for the teams to keep and organise their documentation in one place. Confluence provide you an option to keep your documentation repository in the cloud or on your server.

The content in Confluence is organised in spaces. Depending on the nature of your project, you can choose from several types of spaces. For example, team space or knowledge base space. 

At the heart of Confluence is a text editor. Each individual piece of content is called a page in in Confluence. The suite of documents in your workspace can be organised in a tree like fashion by creating sub-pages.In addition, you assign a label to a page. Labels provide you with a great flexibility to access you documents by the subject matter rather then location. 

Members of your team can mark a particular page to be notified of updates.

When creating a new page, you can set permissions to ensure ==secure access==. 

Confluence provides integration with Trello and Jira, which makes it very popular with the software teams. What's more, there are numerous numerous opportunities for customization. Atlassian market place lists huge amount of apps to add functionalities to your documentation in many categories, from document appearance to project management.

Confluence allows lots of customization. You can attach media files and text files to your pages, you can create information panels you can insert timelines and other data from other application in the add last en suite call ma you can insert code snippets and markdown text, dates and tables, in your Confluence editor.

For the most common types of documents Confluence proposal a number of useful templates to use which saves you time and organisers your documents.

Confluence allows a version control. Every time you  your publish new version of a page, Confluence creates a new version of a page. You can compare different versions and restoring all the versions if necessary.

When you when you are ready to publish your page you can choose several types of publishing your page: if you feel your page needs more revision, you can publish without notifying the watchers. If your page is ready to be shared with the others you can press publish button. In this case the new version of your page is created.

Confluence is really very powerful collaborative tool. But it needs some time to learn about all the features.

## Focus To-Do:Focus Timer & Tasks

Focus to do is a time management app.{ which can help you to get your job done in time. }

Focus to the is based on Pomodoro technique. Pomodoro technique was invented in 1990s by a developer and entrepreneur Francesco  Cirillo.

The idea of behind is to break up your daily tasks into short time intervals during which you are going focus on your work.Pomodoro technique calls for short breaks between focused intervals to help you to stay productive longer.

With Focus To-Do you can create projects for you work and assign tasks and subtasks for the project and estimate the time each task is going to take. You can then set the date for your tasks, repetition and reminders to make sure you don't miss anything. The app have a flexible option for delivery date, called "Some day". 

Once you have a set of tasks for today and decided on the length of your focused intervals and breaks, you can start to focus a chosen task. The app will act as a timer, notifying you of the end of each interval.

Pomodoro technique was designed to help you to train yourself gradually to stay focused and improve your concentration. 

It has relatively simple and intuitive interface, is very flexible and it’s free.
